What's Formula 1 ?

Formula 1 is a open-wheel single seater racing car championship all around the world. There is 1 season per year, and approximatively 22 weeks of race. 1 week begin with free Practice, then qualification and the race whose called Grand Prix in Formula 1.

1 week in Formula 1

A week in F1 is 3 days straight of racing. It begin the Friday with The Free Practice 1 & 2. Then the Saturday before the Qualification there is a final Free Practice. And the Race take place the Sunday.

Free Practice

Free practice are a opportunity for the teams to test and refine their car setup and is a great way to test the new upgrades. As for the driver, they use Free Practice to get familiar with the track and push their limits.
